Viking Stew

This rich and flavorful stew is made with slow-cooked meats, root vegetables, and mix of spices. This recipe is perfect for the cold winter nights.

Ingredients

  • 1lb beef or venison
  • 2 onions
  • 3 carrots
  • 2 parsnips
  • 2 turnips
  • 4 cups beef broth
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1 tsp dried sage
  • 1 tsb dried rosemary

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, add the meat and water/broth, bringing it to a boil.
  2. Reduce heat and let it simmer for 1 hour, skimming off any foam.
  3. Add chopped vegetables, and herbs.
  4. Simmer for another 45 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Season with salt and pepper, then serve hot.
